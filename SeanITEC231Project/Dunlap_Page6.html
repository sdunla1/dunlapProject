The future is unforseeable. What we do know is that monetary costs will eventually rule the new censorship "game" in the United States.

Governments and companies alike will outsource to private organizations in order to monitor and censor sites across the web.

So, if you are an individual who is not comfortable with the current level of censorship, there is hope for diversity in the future. Censorship will become a luxury more than a form of protection.
