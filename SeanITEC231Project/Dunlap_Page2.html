Censorship comes from the Latin word censere, which means for an individual to give their opinion

These opinions are assessments of how people perceive the world around them. Opinions are shared across an array of differentiating platforms:

  1. Music
  2. Television
  3. Newspaper
  4. Magazines
  5. Social Networks
