Censorship is nothing new, even internationally! There are numerous historical events that detail the governing of censorship, dating back to even before the 14th century.

Notably, The Royal Shambles was a sarcastic and humorous print in london that, as you can guess, was not approved by the british government.

The print ran from 1770 to 1830, covering topics of good taste in food, social graces, and even sexual propriety.
